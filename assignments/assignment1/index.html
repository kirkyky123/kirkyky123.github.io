Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online video game developed by Epic Games.
It is known for its battle royale mode, where players fight each other until only one remains.
The game features a colorful world, with a variety of weapons, items, and a unique building mechanic that adds depth to the gameplay.
Fortnite also has one of the if not the biggest multiverse of any show, movie, or game in history.

The success of Fortnite is due to the easy but high skill ceiling game mechanics combined with its visuals.
Being able to play as Spiderman, Eminem, LeBron James, Naruto, or thousands of other iconic characters in the same game is unheard of and plays a big role in Fortnite's success.
It is available on multiple platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices, allowing players to connect and play with friends regardless of their gaming platform.
Additionally, Epic Games regularly releases new content, such as limited-time events, new game modes, and cosmetic items, keeping the game fresh for its large player base.
